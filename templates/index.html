Threat Coverage

Threats We Detect

Our AI model is trained on millions of real-world attack patterns and continuously updated with emerging threat intelligence.

Tech Support Scams
Fake alerts claiming your device is infected, designed to trick you into calling scammers or granting remote access.
Credential Harvesting
Lookalike login pages impersonating banks, Google, Microsoft, and other trusted services to steal your username and password.
Fake Surveys & Prizes
Deceptive survey pages and fake prize notifications that harvest personal data or push malware downloads.
Delivery Impersonation
Spoofed shipping notifications from FedEx, DHL, or Amazon asking you to "verify" payment or personal details.
Malware Distribution
Sites hosting ransomware, spyware, trojans, or drive-by download scripts that execute silently in your browser.
Crypto Scams
Fake investment platforms, rug-pull token sites, cryptojacking pages, and wallet-draining smart contract links.
Banking Phishing
Pixel-perfect clones of banking portals designed to capture account credentials and one-time passwords in real time.
Email Link Attacks
Malicious links embedded in HTML emails that use URL obfuscation, redirectors, or shortened URLs to evade filters.
