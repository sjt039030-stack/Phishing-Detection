How It Works

Deep Learning-Based Phishing Detection

Our system uses a Deep Neural Network (DNN) enhanced with batch normalization to classify URLs as legitimate or malicious in real time. It analyses structural features — URL length, domain patterns, subdomain depth, special characters — alongside behavioural indicators like redirect chains and SSL certificate anomalies.

By leveraging intelligent pattern recognition and continuous model retraining on fresh threat intelligence feeds, PhishGuard adapts to evolving attack techniques, maintaining a false-positive rate below 0.03% while catching over 99.8% of confirmed phishing attempts.
